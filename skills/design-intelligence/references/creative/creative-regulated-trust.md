# Creative Reference Pack: Regulated Trust

Reference role: platform standard
Quality tier: tier-2 local curated pack
Best for: fast path, medium path, full creative path

Creative moves:
- Create trust through clarity, restraint, recovery, proof, plain language, and
  accessibility-safe novelty.
- Use visual identity in service of comprehension: contrast, hierarchy,
  predictable controls, and explicit consequences.
- Treat risk states as premium surfaces, not afterthoughts.

Borrow:
- Finance, healthcare, legal, government, insurance, education for minors,
  security, and compliance-sensitive products.
- Review-before-submit, undo, escalation, audit, and support visibility.
- Calm motion, conservative density, and proof blocks.

Avoid:
- Decorative novelty that weakens comprehension.
- Ambiguous claims, hidden fees, weak error recovery, and low-contrast trust
  theater.
- Treating a regulated domain like a generic lifestyle campaign.

Differentiation pressure:
- Use when competitors all look conservative but still need a memorable,
  defensible brand move.
- Combine with data products for expert dashboards and with luxury for
  high-value trust surfaces.

Do not use as style authority:
- This pack provides pattern ingredients, not a product to copy.
