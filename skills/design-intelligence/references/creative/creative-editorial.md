# Creative Reference Pack: Editorial Systems

Reference role: creative benchmark
Quality tier: tier-2 local curated pack
Best for: medium path, full creative path

Creative moves:
- Build hierarchy through rhythm, pull-quote scale, paced white space, and
  confident editorial sequencing.
- Use type contrast and image cropping to create narrative tension without
  needing decorative UI.
- Treat each section as an argument with a lead, proof, pause, and close.

Borrow:
- Magazine-like sequencing for landing pages, launch narratives, research
  reports, portfolios, and expert products.
- Strong typographic contrast where content has a real point of view.
- Section pacing that lets proof blocks feel curated instead of stacked.

Avoid:
- Turning every operational UI into a magazine spread.
- Hiding scan-critical labels, states, or controls behind expressive layout.
- Copying a publication's style instead of adapting hierarchy and rhythm.

Differentiation pressure:
- Use editorial confidence when the category is full of template SaaS pages.
- Pair with data-products or regulated-trust packs when evidence must remain
  fast to scan.

Do not use as style authority:
- This pack provides pattern ingredients, not a product to copy.
