# Architecture Decision Record Template

Use this reference-only template when a durable engineering decision needs enough context for future maintainers to understand why an option was accepted or rejected. Prefer the active decision brief template for user-facing product choices.

## Options Considered

Record the viable options, including the option selected and at least one rejected alternative. Keep tradeoffs concrete: operational cost, complexity cost, rollout risk, rollback path, support burden, and user impact.

## Decision

State the selected option and the reason it fits the current product, architecture, data, security, performance, and support constraints.

## Consequences

List expected benefits, accepted risks, migration or deprecation work, observability needs, and known follow-ups.

## Verification

Record the exact commands, reviews, runtime checks, or evidence artifacts that prove the decision is safe enough to proceed.
